Can I change my monthly plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account. The new rate applies on the next billing cycle.

Are my donations tax-deductible?

We email monthly receipts you can use for tax purposes. You can also download past receipts from your account.

How often do I receive updates?

You’ll receive enrolment, progress note, health check and term results updates throughout the sponsorship journey.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel from your account settings. Your support continues until the end of the current billing cycle.
